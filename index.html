Visualization of Delicious.com References

For 8 years I have enlisted students in my Innovation course to locate references to articles related to "Innovation" and "Diffusion" (the spread of an innovation). These references have been posted to the web platform delicious.com (formerly del.icio.us) under a single username for each year. OUR GOALS

To examining content from the delicious.com platform as links to articles contributed from students enrolled in my Innovation courses each year between 2007 and this term (excluding 2013). The delicious API, that we’ll use... https://github.com/SciDevs/delicious-api
 

Primary Goal:
To take all of these individual links, build an internal dataset, and develop visualizations that show the trends and interests of students looking for “innovation” over this time period.

Secondary Goal:
To build a platform that can continue to develop these visualizations iteratively with each new year.
